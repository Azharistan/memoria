About Us

Our Story

In Memorial was conceived from a deeply personal experience with loss by founders Ben Pelta and Behzad Qazi. When Ben faced the passing of a cherished family member, he found solace in crafting a digital tribute filled with photos, stories, and treasured moments. This act not only aided in processing grief but also brought his family closer together.

Recognizing the disconnection between digital memorials and the physical world, Ben and his friends envisioned a solution: linking QR codes to digital tributes for instant access at gravesites, homes, and memorial altars. This idea sparked the creation of In Memorial, a company dedicated to blending technology with compassion to create meaningful tributes.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: Our goal is to reshape the grieving process by offering innovative, technology-driven memorial solutions that celebrate life and preserve memories in accessible, meaningful ways.

Vision: We aspire to a world where memories are not only preserved but celebrated interactively. By bridging traditional memorialization with the digital era, we aim to provide comfort and connection to grieving families worldwide.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach every service with empathy and respect.
  • Innovation: We continually push the boundaries of memorial technology.
  • Accessibility: We make it easy to create and share memorial tributes.
  • Quality: We deliver premium products and experiences.

What We Offer

Core Product

Our flagship offering is a customizable online memorial page, accessible through QR codes linked to physical memorials. These pages include features like unlimited images and videos, guest books, family tree integration, and fundraising options.

AI-Enhanced Features

We provide advanced AI capabilities, such as voice cloning and animated avatars, to create immersive and interactive tributes. Our "My Memory Will Live On" program allows individuals to pre-train an AI to create a realistic avatar for posthumous interaction.

Memorial Products

We offer a variety of physical items with QR codes, including candles, jewelry, framed photos, and memorial stones, creating a seamless connection between physical and digital tributes.
