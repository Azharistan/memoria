Process

Easy Account Creation

Setting up a memorial page is simple and intuitive. Families can easily create an account and begin crafting a tribute that reflects the life and legacy of their loved one.

Content Upload

Add photos, videos, and stories to create a rich tapestry of memories. Our platform allows for easy uploading and organization of content, ensuring that every tribute is as unique as the individual it honors.

QR Code Integration

Once the memorial page is complete, receive a unique QR code that links directly to the tribute. This code can be placed on physical memorials, allowing for instant access to the digital tribute at any time.
